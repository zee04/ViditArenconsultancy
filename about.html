About Chef Vidit Aren

Vidit Aren is a distinguished chef and culinary leader based in Mumbai, renowned for his pivotal roles at acclaimed establishments such as Slink & Bardot and Soufflé S'il Vous Plaît, where he served as Head Chef and Managing Partner, respectively.

With over 14 years of experience in catering to A-list celebrities and top-tier corporates, he has established himself as a visionary in the culinary world.

Our Philosophy

We are a boutique Chef consultancy dedicated to curate each project personally by Chef Vidit Aren. His passion for global cuisines and innovative techniques allows us to create unique experiences that genuinely resonate with our clients.

We take pride in our commitment to quality and artistry, aiming to make every project memorable. By focusing on personalised service, we strive to offer solutions that truly reflect your brand and foster meaningful connections with your audience.

Our approach is rooted in humility and collaboration, ensuring that we work closely with you to achieve your vision.
